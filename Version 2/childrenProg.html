Some of the topics covered in the children program

How to pray?

Children, they don't know what to say to God when asked to pray and and how to say it. At Little Ark Ministries we teach children how to pray. We use various object lessons like; the pumpkin prayer and Oreo prayer.

How to read or study the Bible?

The Bible at times is a big and scary book where even mature students/parents don't know where to start. We, Little Ark came up with exciting methods to make the reading of the Bible fun and exciting while learning. We use methods and programs like: * The Alphabets of the Bible * The Mnemonics of the Bible * Who said That? Our books are designed in a manner that children navigate the Bible while they're looking for answers when completing the worksheets. Any many more!
