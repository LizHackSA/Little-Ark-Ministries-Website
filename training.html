Who Do We Train?

Christianity is the basis of all educations. The Lord is at heart of the community and all children are welcome. Every child is valued and every child is known and loved and success is shared. Co-operation is preferred to competition. Compassion and forgiveness are encouraged, and difficulties are talked though. The day begins and ends in prayer.
