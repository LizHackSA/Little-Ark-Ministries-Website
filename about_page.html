What is Little Ark Ministries?

Who Do We Train?

Little Ark Ministries is a Non-Profit Organization which trains children from the age of three to ten years of age to develop Christian principles and make them disciples of Christ in their generation (Matt. 28:16-20). We are not only addressing or reaching out other children from perspective of religion or spiritual point of view but also from ethical and moral point of view. We are Christian centred religious community dedicated and serving God, sharing the Gospel and helping others. We are Christians who are members of Khulani Nkululeko NPO. Little Ark Ministries are children who take great joy in living according to Christ example of sacrifice and humility. We are Christians who share in the joy of:

  • Serving God through a daily relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Waiting for Christ’s glorious return
  • Helping people at home and around us in Christ name
  • Leading an active, healthy Christian life!

Wherein doth the good education of children consist?

  • In the tender and careful nursing of them.
  • In bringing them to be baptized.
  • In a due care to inform and instruct them in the whole compass of their duty to God and to their neighbour.
  • In a prudent/practical and diligent care to form their lives and manners to religion and virtue.
  • In giving them good example.
  • In wise restraints from that which is evil; by seasonable reproof and correction.
  • In bringing them to be confirmed and content.

Little Ark saw that religion has taken over of our responsibilities and yes, in contrary religion should stimulate us to do what we supposed to do as Christians. In other words going to church makes us to go to the needy. What qualifies us to do this, it is not by qualification (degree or doctorate) but by the fact that we too have lost our loved ones, grew up in single parent homes, and we have friends and family members who are affected by HIV and AIDS, hence we sympathise with them. We have developed programs that will stimulate them physically, mentally and spiritually. Little Ark Ministries feels that NOW is the time to go out, to touch and change someone’s life (Prov. 22:6). We don’t ask too much (John’s head), all we want is a child just to get into the boat. As soon as possible every child should be led to the knowledge of the Saviour (Gen. 14:14, Eph. 6:4).

Three promises

1. The Bible alone will be your textbook!
2. Jesus Christ will be uplifted-Our hope in these last days!
3. You will enjoy studying your Bible and learn more than you may have in your entire life!

Our goal

Is to see our children as they grow up to follow and see Jesus as their role model

How do we hope to achieve these goals?

We meet every Sunday morning for an hour or two. We are going through 40 lessons in class with in the period of a year (see our work book). However, we also have extra material which is provided during holidays like the Exodus, Moses, Joshua stories and the Sanctuary and its services (see our work book).
